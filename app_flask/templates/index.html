Features

What your report includes.

More than a number — a complete view of your financial position.

Credit Score Estimate
A predicted score from 300 to 900 based on your income, savings, debt, spending, and repayment history — mapped to industry-standard bands.
Default Risk Level
A probability-based assessment of whether your profile suggests low, medium, or high lending risk — with a clear approve, review, or decline signal.
Key Financial Ratios
Savings rate, EMI burden, expense ratio, and debt-to-income — the four ratios lenders look at first when evaluating an application.
Spending Breakdown
A visual breakdown of where your money goes each month, highlighting categories that may be affecting your overall financial health.
Improvement Plan
Specific, prioritised actions based on your actual profile — not generic advice. Each recommendation is tied directly to a factor affecting your score.
Instant and Private
Your analysis runs entirely within this application. No data is stored, shared, or sent to any third party.
